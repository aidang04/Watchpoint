Fold the paper vertically to make a crease in the middle and then return to normal.
Fold the edges to meet in the middle, it should look like a kite.
Fold the edges to meet in the middle again.
Fold the bottom corner up to the top corner.
Fold the top of what you just folded down to make the head.
Fold the entire model in half, making sure the head is still on top.
Pull the neck upwards and flatten the paper.
Do the same for the head.
Enjoy your swan!
