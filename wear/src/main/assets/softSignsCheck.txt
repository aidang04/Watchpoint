Keep count of how many soft signs are recognised.
Are they less talkative?
Lost interest in things they normally enjoy?
Seem more anxious, agitated or withdrawn?
Are they moving slower than yesterday?
Are they reluctant to get up & move?
Do they seem more unsteady on their feet?
Did they eat their usual amount today?
Are they drinking enough?
Any new difficulty eating?
Has their skin colour changed? (e.g. more pale)
Are they grimacing, guarding or rubbing an area?
Do they seem uncomfortable when at rest?
Are they more confused than usual?
Are they responding to you more slowly than usual?
Have they said anything unusual?
1-2 signs: Monitor closely and check again in a few hrs.
3-4 signs: Contact GP today.
5+ signs: Call 111 or GP immediately.
